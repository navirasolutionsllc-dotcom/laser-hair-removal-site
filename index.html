About the Clinic

AuraVera Aesthetic Institute is a physician-led aesthetic clinic in Hilliard, Ohio, specializing in safe, effective laser hair removal. Located in the Columbus metropolitan area, AuraVera stands out because all laser procedures are performed directly by Dr. Amir Dada, DO—a licensed physician—ensuring the highest level of care and safety.

We use the Candela GentleMax Pro, a dual-wavelength laser system trusted for its precision, comfort, and ability to treat all skin types, including darker skin tones.

Our Laser Technology

At AuraVera, we use the Candela GentleMax Pro, recognized as the gold standard in laser hair removal for its precision, versatility, and consistent patient satisfaction. Its dual‑wavelength system (755 nm Alexandrite + 1064 nm Nd:YAG) enables safe and effective results for all skin types and hair textures.

Dr. Amir Dada personally performs every session, ensuring impeccable safety, customized settings, and high-quality outcomes—never delegated to non-medical staff.

The GentleMax Pro features a Dynamic Cooling Device™ (DCD) that sprays cryogen before each laser pulse to maximize comfort and protect your skin—reducing side effects while improving the overall experience.

Clients often see up to 80% hair reduction after just three sessions, with minimal downtime and long-lasting smoothness.
